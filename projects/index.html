Go Game Clock

Android (native)

A niche clock app for keeping track of player times in the board game Go.

The app has support for complicated "byoyomi" time controls that are common in Go but which other game clock apps (usually tailored for chess) do not support.

Currently with 1300+ downloads and a 4.5-star rating on Google Play.

Chess Engine

C++

A computer engine for the game of chess. This engine does not have a graphical interface, but instead uses the text-based UCI protocol, allowing it to be installed and run on nearly every chess GUI program.

The engine's AI uses a min-max tree search with alpha-beta pruning. To evaluate positions, it applies basic strategies like counting material and trying to gauge the effectiveness of individual pieces.

The engine's overall chess skill is slightly stronger than that of a human beginner, though further improvements are in the works.

External Projects with Major Contributions

Manifold

Java

Manifold is an open-source hardware description language developed at the University of Waterloo. It is designed to be high-level, easy-to-use, domain-agnostic, and similar to modern functional programming languages.

As part of my fourth-year design project at the University of Waterloo, I worked with some fellow undergraduate and graduate students to extend Manifold. We focused on giving Manifold the ability to describe and analyze microfluidic circuits. Microfluidic circuits are an immature domain and our group aimed to make Manifold the first ever end-to-end automated toolchain for microfluidic circuit design. We ended up making a prototype that can handle trivial circuits.

My main individual contribution to Manifold was adding the ability to export system schematics in the Modelica model format.

Our group got joint-first place at the 2017 UW software engineering capstone symposium. We also wrote a research paper on our work which was accepted for the 2017 IEEE Canadian Conference on Electrical and Computer Engineering. We presented our paper at CCECE 2017 in Windsor, ON in May 2017.
